Project Overview

Our Solution

Our Aquavolt project represents a breakthrough in renewable energy technology. By creating a Pelton turbine, we generate clean electricity through a specially designed turbine system that maximizes energy extraction while minimizing environmental impact.

The prototype utilizes an Arduino-based monitoring system with current and voltage sensors to collect real-time data from the DC motor. This data helps us analyze efficiency, power output, and optimize the turbine design for various water flow conditions.

This dashboard provides real-time monitoring capabilities and documentation of our testing process, showcasing our innovative approach to sustainable energy generation.
